Brandon Gasser

NDSU Computer Science Student

My name is Brandon Gasser. I am from Albertville, Minnesota. I graduated high school and started college at NDSU as a computer science major in 2021. I am now a junior in computer science. I was active on my high school's FIRST Robotics team and now I am active in Bison Robotics. Now, I volunteer at FIRST Robotics Competition events as a Control Systems Advisor. I am also in the ACM at NDSU. At the ACM, I lead a special interest group on functional programming.

In the future, I would like to have a job near Minneapolis so that I don't have to move far from home. I would like to be working on backend software for a business or robotics.
